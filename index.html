Why “Liquid”?

Good ideas take the shape of the problem.

Most AI projects fail the same way: a rigid solution gets forced onto a real, messy business. We work the other way around. We start with how your work actually flows — the emails, the spreadsheets, the Tuesday-afternoon bottleneck — and pour the technology into that shape.

The light bulb in the glass is our reminder. An idea is only worth something once it’s held by something real.
